FAQ

Support

Does Snapport Lite guarantee my photo will be accepted?

No. Photo requirements vary by country, agency, and application type. Snapport Lite provides common templates and guides — it does not guarantee acceptance and is not affiliated with any government. Always review the official requirements before submitting.

Which templates are included?

Snapport Lite includes common templates for the US, UK, Schengen/EU, Japan, Korea, Taiwan, China, Canada, Australia, India, plus ID card, student ID, resume, and work badge photos.

Can I create a custom size?

Yes. You can create custom sizes in millimeters, inches, or pixels, with any DPI.

Are my photos uploaded anywhere?

No. Snapport Lite processes photos on your iPhone with Apple Vision, person segmentation, Core Image, and PDFKit. There is no account, no cloud processing, no ads, and no tracking.

What is Check for Template Updates?

It is optional and user-initiated. When you tap it, Snapport Lite downloads a public JSON list of common photo sizes and sends no personal data.

What export formats are supported?

Snapport Lite exports JPEG, PNG, and PDF, and can create print-ready 4×6, A4, and Letter sheets with cut guides.

Is Snapport Lite a subscription?

No. Every template and editing tool is available, and your first successful export is free. One US$4.99 lifetime unlock enables unlimited additional exports. No subscription, ads, or tracking.

Which devices and languages are supported?

Snapport Lite supports iPhone and iPad on iOS 17 or later. The app declares all 50 App Store locales.
